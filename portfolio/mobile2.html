Product Recon Mobile

Product Recon iOS App

Main aim of iOS app was to give users an ability to audit products by going to store. Administrator can assign various missions to user. User will have to go to store in order to complete the mission. The app is complex in its own way and allows full fledged features like Barcode scan, audit history, audit status, notifications, mission due notifications etc. It also gives an option to mark product if not in stock/not going to be stocked forever by store owners.

Technologies/Tools:

  • Xamarin Studio
  • Xamarin iOS
  • C#
  • ASP .NET Web Api
